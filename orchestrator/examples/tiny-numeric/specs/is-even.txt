=== TASK_DESCRIPTION ===

Return true exactly when a natural number is evenly divisible by two. Zero is
even. A value is even when its remainder after division by two is zero. The
method is pure, deterministic, and total for every natural number.

=== METHOD_SIGNATURE ===

method isEven(value: Nat) returns (result: Bool)

=== TEST_CASES ===

{
  "test_1": {"input": {"value": 0}, "expected": true},
  "test_2": {"input": {"value": 1}, "expected": false},
  "test_3": {"input": {"value": 2}, "expected": true},
  "test_4": {"input": {"value": 17}, "expected": false},
  "test_5": {"input": {"value": 100}, "expected": true}
}
